About Us

Story Telling

Snow Junk is Kolkata's local homemade ice cream brand - which is preservative-free, egg-less, and hence, a healthier choice. Due to the undeniable truth that the experience of indulging in ice creams elevates every moment to a higher level of enjoyment, we find ourselves filled with immense delight and satisfaction in crafting our delectable creations that bring smiles and happiness to the faces of those who savor them. Our ultimate aspiration is to capture and win over the hearts of as many individuals as possible, spreading joy and sweetness through each delightful scoop.
